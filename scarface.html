The Brief

The objective was to create a luxury 3-slide Instagram carousel promoting seamless stays and untamed adventures in Kenya and Tanzania. The aesthetic needed to be "Royal African" — balancing the rawness of the wild with the comfort of high-end travel.

Project Tech Stack

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Font: Neulis & Poppins

The Solution

We designed a seamless narrative across three slides. Slide 1 establishes the hook ("Stay in Royalty"), Slide 2 showcases the experience ("Curated Hideaways"), and Slide 3 drives the action ("Book Now").

Campaign Copywriting

"An African safari isn’t just a holiday — it’s a story you live. With Scarface Pride, you travel like royalty, sleep like a native, and collect memories wild enough to brag about."
